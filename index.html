Welcome to the world of personality!

In this course, you will learn about personality in the form of factor analysis, and implemented as a structural equation model (SEM).

You'll learn the five factor model, and much more about the causes and consequences of personality.

At the end you will be comfortable:

  1. Knowing what personality measures assess, and different modalities of test
  2. Applications such as predicting health or income.
  3. Reasoning about why we behave as we do.
  4. Testing theories of personality by constructing "steel man" rather than "straw man" comparisons
